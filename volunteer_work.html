Extra-curricular Activities and Volunteer Work

I have been involved with different groups, sports teams, and organizing committees outside of regular course requirements. These experiences have given me a profound sense of freedom and belief that hard work and commitment should go beyond the classroom and office. I have made lifelong friends while being part of these different teams.

Volunteer Work

Clemson University

  • Reviewed manuscripts for the IEEE - EMBS 16th International Conference on Wearable and Implantable Body Sensor Networks & the 2020 25th International Conference on Pattern Recognition.
  • Served as the Graduate Student Senator and representative of the Electrical and Computer Engineering department in the Graduate Student Government (GSG) at Clemson University from Aug. 2018 to Jan. 2019.

Maharashtra Institute of Technology - Pune

  • Worked as a Co-Researcher at the department of Computer Engineering (Feb. 2018 - Jul. 2018), under the supervision of Prof. Madhura Phatak. Our research focused on developing deep learning models that could predict a numerical score for the aesthetic quality of images and videos.
  • Served as a judge for the technical competition Techzephyr - 2018 at M.I.T - Pune for different engineering projects related to image processing and pattern recognition.
  • As a Graduate Student Volunteer at the department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (Jan. 2014 - Apr. 2014), I was involved with preparing teaching material, conducting laboratory exercises, and grading student reports for undergraduate students.
  • Served as the Head of the Medical Assistance Committee (2010 - 2011) at SUMMIT, the annual Inter-Engineering National Level Sports Meet held at M.I.T. - Pune. I was responsible for arranging first-aid and emergency medical services for all participating athletes.
