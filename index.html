About me

Hi, I'm Yadnyesh, an electronics engineer, machine learning researcher, and software programmer. I grew up in the city of Pune, India, and have briefly lived in Delft, in The Netherlands. Currently, I'm staying in Dearborn, Michigan, enjoying the quiet lifestyle that its residents seem to like as well.

My academic journey started as an Electronics & Telecommunication Engineer at M.I.T - Pune in India, where I was introduced to the field of artificial intelligence. My first project classified the handwritten script on postal envelopes into two categories, English and the local script Devanagari. I-PASS, as it was called, was meant to assist in sorting envelopes at local postal offices.

Through my education and professional experience, I've built a strong foundation in applied mathematics, optimization, software programming, and statistical analysis. I also have solid programming skills in Python and C, including system programming, bash scripting, and deep learning model design using the TensorFlow and PyTorch APIs.
